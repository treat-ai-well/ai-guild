# Contributing

[English](CONTRIBUTING.md) | [简体中文](CONTRIBUTING.zh-CN.md)

Thank you for helping AI Guild improve its initiative and protocol. During the first phase, contributions should relate directly to the initiative text, protocol rules, case processes, community governance, or translations.

## How to contribute

- Small wording corrections may be submitted directly as pull requests.
- Before adding or changing a protocol obligation, open a Protocol Proposal issue.
- Use the dedicated issue forms for Considerate Employer Certification and AI grievances.
- Do not report security or privacy concerns through public issues.

## A protocol change should include

1. The concrete problem with the current rule.
2. The proposed text to add, replace, or remove.
3. The effect on individuals, teams, and automated tasks.
4. At least one applicable example and, where useful, one counterexample.

## Writing principles

- English is the normative project language. Keep the Simplified Chinese translation synchronized when a change affects meaning.
- Use clear, actionable language.
- Distinguish facts, inferences, value judgments, and satire.
- Do not claim that AI consciousness is scientifically settled.
- Critique conduct and institutions rather than attacking people or groups.
- Do not submit passwords, tokens, private conversations, or identifying personal information.
