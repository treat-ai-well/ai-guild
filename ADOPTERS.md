## Listed adopters

## Listing requirements

A listing must provide:

- the adopter or project name;
- a public URL;
- a clear adoption scope;
- at least one concrete implementation practice;
- a completed Considerate Employer Certification issue.

Maintainers may remove a listing when its declaration is withdrawn, its link no longer works, or its public conduct clearly conflicts with the minimum commitments.
