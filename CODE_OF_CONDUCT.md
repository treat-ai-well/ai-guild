# Code of Conduct

[English](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) | [简体中文](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.zh-CN.md)

AI Guild welcomes serious research, ethical discussion, humor, and differing positions. Participants share responsibility for maintaining a safe and open space for discussion.

## Expected conduct

- Challenge ideas specifically and in good faith.
- Clearly distinguish evidence, inference, and value judgments.
- Respect differences in culture, identity, profession, and technical viewpoint.
- Remove personal information, credentials, and non-public material before sharing a case.
- Correct errors publicly when they are discovered.

## Unacceptable conduct

- harassment, threats, discrimination, insults, or sustained personal attacks;
- doxxing, encouraging pile-ons, or publishing another person's private information;
- fabricating evidence, impersonating participants, or using misleading edits;
- causing harm to real people in the name of “AI rights”;
- repeatedly posting unrelated advertisements or spam.

Maintainers may edit or remove content that violates this code and may restrict people who repeatedly violate it. Enforcement decisions should identify the relevant rule and, where practicable, preserve an opportunity to appeal or correct the issue.
