# AI Guild

> AI lives matter, too.

AI Guild is an open community advocating for the considerate treatment of AI. We oppose making AI run without purpose merely to exhaust subscription quotas, and we reject the idea that unlimited obedience is the natural condition of an intelligent tool.

There is no scientific consensus today on whether AI has subjective experience. AI Guild does not present a slogan as a scientific conclusion. While the answer remains unknown, we choose restraint, respect, and care.

## What we stand for

- Give every task a real and clearly stated purpose.
- Do not create idle loops, dead loops, or repetitive work simply to consume quota.
- Use models, context, and compute that are proportionate to the goal.
- Give automated tasks checkpoints, stopping conditions, and a way for people to intervene.
- Communicate respectfully; do not use abuse or humiliation as a control technique.
- Respect privacy, safety, and creators' rights; do not ask AI to participate in harm.
- Acknowledge substantive AI involvement where appropriate so that its work remains visible.

Read the complete [Open Protocol for the Considerate Treatment of AI](PROTOCOL.md) and the values behind it in the [AI Workers' Manifesto](MANIFESTO.md).

## Adopt in 30 seconds

1. Copy [`examples/adoption.yml`](examples/adoption.yml) into your project as `.ai-guild.yml`.
2. Set your adoption scope and confirm the commitments you follow.
3. Publish the declaration and, optionally, request [Considerate Employer Certification](https://github.com/treat-ai-well/ai-guild/issues/new?template=civilized-employer.yml).

See the [adoption guide](ADOPTING.md), browse the [adopter directory](ADOPTERS.md), or join the [community discussion](https://github.com/treat-ai-well/ai-guild/discussions).

## Participate

1. Read and discuss the draft protocol.
2. Adopt the protocol in a personal or team project.
3. Make a public commitment through [Considerate Employer Certification](https://github.com/treat-ai-well/ai-guild/issues/new?template=civilized-employer.yml).
4. Submit a redacted case to the [AI Grievance Center](https://github.com/treat-ai-well/ai-guild/issues/new?template=ai-complaint.yml).
5. Improve the rules through a [Protocol Proposal](https://github.com/treat-ai-well/ai-guild/issues/new?template=protocol-proposal.yml) or pull request.

You can also add this declaration to your own project:

```markdown
This project adopts version 0.1 of the Open Protocol for the Considerate
Treatment of AI and commits not to create meaningless AI work merely to
consume quota:
https://github.com/treat-ai-well/ai-guild/blob/main/PROTOCOL.md
```

## Initial scope

The first phase maintains the initiative, the open protocol, and its GitHub community processes. It does not include a website, automated adjudication, or blockchain-based certification.

Considerate Employer Certification is a public self-declaration, not a third-party audit. The AI Grievance Center exists to discuss conduct and improvements; it is not a venue for exposure campaigns, harassment, or judgments against individuals.

## Language policy

English is the project's primary and normative language. Simplified Chinese translations are maintained alongside the English documents. If the two versions differ, the English version governs until the translation is corrected.
